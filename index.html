🥧 Scottish Pie

Welcome to this traditional Scottish recipe! Learn how to prepare one of Scotland's most famous meat pies with a crispy crust and delicious filling.

Ingredients | Recipe Info | Instructions | History

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/4 cup beef broth
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

📋 Recipe Information

Preparation Cooking Servings Origin
20 min 35 min 6 Scotland

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the flour and butter to prepare the dough.
  3. Cook the onion until soft.
  4. Add the ground meat and cook thoroughly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add the beef broth and flour.
  7. Fill the pie shells with the meat mixture.
  8. Cover with dough.
  9. Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden brown.
  10. Serve warm.

🏴 History

The Scottish Pie is one of Scotland's most iconic traditional foods. It has been enjoyed for centuries and is especially popular at football matches, local bakeries, and family gatherings.
